How it works:

    1. Click anywhere to reset cellular automata everytime sound is adjusted.
    2. Top row of buttons toggles sounds. Some sounds may take a few seconds to start playing.
    3. Top row of sliders controls amplitude. Watch how the cellular automata changes depending on amplitude.
    4. Bottom row of buttons toggles notes/chords.
    5. Bottom row of sliders controls cellular automata's speed in playing notes.
    6. Sounds can be overlayed with or without chords. Only one chord can be played at a time.
